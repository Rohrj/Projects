User Flow

When promoting the products, focus will be on fulfilling the following four main goals from four main entry points:

  • Google Search for "vintage jewelry"
    • Views top search results
      • Clicks on company website link and Lands on homepage
        • Views displayed information on products and top reviews
          • Navigates to shop catalog
            • Views all jewelry and reads reviews
              • Clicks on a set of earrings
                • Purchase selected set of earrings
                • Review the product they purchased
  • Etsy
    • Searches for "vintage minks"
      • Visits Enchanté Vintage & Studio's page
        • Clicks on company website in the description
          • Lands on catalog page
            • Views all minks and reads reviews
              • Clicks on a fur mink of their choice
                • Purchase selected mink
                • Share to Facebook

  • Hears about Enchanté Vintage & Studio from a Friend
    • Google searches "Enchanté Vintage & Studio"
      • Views top search results
        • Clicks on company website link and Lands on homepage
          • Views displayed information on products and top reviews
            • Navigates to contact page
              • Fills out a form to ask about a product's quality
                • Recieves a response from the owner
  • QR Code
    • Recieves business card from owner
      • Scans QR code on the card
        • Code directs to Enchanté Vintage & Studio's website
          • Lands on catalog page
            • Sees something a friend would like
              • Share the site with the friend
