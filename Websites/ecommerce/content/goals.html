Audience Goals

Upon entering the site, visitors will:

  1. Purchase a vintage mink on Etsy through our shop catalog
  2. Purchase a vintage set of earrings on Etsy through our shop catalog
  3. Contact the owner if they have questions about the products using our contact us form
  4. Review the product, that they have already purchased, on Etsy
  5. Share the site with their family/friends on Facebook, Pinterest, and Etsy

Analysis of Success

After the site is completed and launched, the following options will be used to analyze success:

  • Which entry point leads to highest percentage of sales.
  • How often customers return to the site.
  • Do returning customers make additional purchases.
  • How many customers are coming to the site from Etsy.
  • Where are customers in the United States.
