Graphic Design

The sites page layout will be responsive, gold-colored, smooth scrolling 1-column layout. The logo will be located in the top right next to the name of company which will help users identify the site. The footer will be a horizontal bar at the bottom of the page and will contain social media icons and links to additional pages. The main navigation bar will be at the top of the page next to the logo/title. This bar will be placed under the icon/title in the tablet view. In the mobile view, this bar will be reduced to a single icon which users can press to expand it.

The color scheme for the site will include company colors converted from CMYK to HLS:
