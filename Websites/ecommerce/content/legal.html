Security and Privacy

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the personal information under our control. User and password information is encrypted before it is transmitted across the network.

We have a firm commitment to privacy. The following statement addresses the information-gathering and dissemination practices for the Enchanté Vintage & Studio website.

In order to purchase items from the online store, we require you provide your name, address, and credit card information. These three items are only stored in a secure database if specified by you. Otherwise, they are strictly used to communicate with you about your order and fulfilling your order. They are then securely deleted. Any data stored in a database will be secured with encrypted passwords. Optional personal data such as phone number and email address will not be saved unless you want to be notified when new items appear in the store. We do not share any personal information with any third parties.

When you visit this website, it automatically collects certain information, such as: Domain, country, IP address, Browser, platform, resolution, Entrance-Exit Pages, Referrals, Date, Time, Search Terms and Search Engines. This is standard practice for websites and is not used for any purpose other than to evaluate how we can design the site to best serve your needs.

In order to provide better and more secure service we will occasionally use a “cookie.” A cookie is a small piece of information stored on your computer that can uniquely identify your browser and may include other information about your visit to the site. The cookie cannot be read by a website other than the one that set the cookie.
